LAB-HA

This is the homepage for the Laboratory of Advanced Biotechnologies for Health Assessment (LAB-HA) at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Lassonde School of Engineering at York University. We are led by our fearless leader Professor Razieh (Neda) Salahandish.

The Laboratory of Advanced Biotechnologies for Health Assessment (LAB-HA) at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science within York University's Lassonde School of Engineering brings together a diverse group of scientists, engineers, and Publicationsers who collaborate closely to develop and apply cutting-edge technologies, such as bioinformatics, artificial intelligence, sensor technologies, and advanced imaging systems, to tackle pressing health challenges. Through pioneering Publications, the laboratory strives to accelerate the translation of scientific discoveries into practical applications, benefiting both patients and healthcare providers.
